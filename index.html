Getting There

Address: 125 Watch Knob Lane, Swannanoa, NC 28778

Important Note: You will likely lose GPS and cell service near the house. Write down your door code on paper and follow these directions carefully!

Directions from Asheville (West)

  • Take Highway 40 East
  • Exit 55 towards US-70
  • Left after you exit towards US-70
  • Right on US-70 (1.7 miles)
  • Left at the gas station onto Warren Wilson Rd (2.8 miles)
  • Sharp Left onto Bee Tree Road (2.3 miles)
  • Right onto Watch Knob Lane
  • You will see a rustic cabin on your right. After the cabin, bear right to stay on Watch Knob Lane. Continue up the gravel road for another half a mile.
  • Best advice for the steep gravel road: Keep your car moving slowly. If you stop on a steep part, you may have to back down and try again.
  • You will pass 2 other homes on your left before you come to a GIANT Rock. Continue past the BIG rock and PRIVATE DRIVE sign to the top.
  • You may park 2-3 cars by the Rapunzel House and 2-3 cars in the driveway leading to the Tree House. If you come to any gates, you are at the wrong house.
  • Enter the front door code to enter! Welcome!!!!
